Welcome to Agha Khan Hospital, a leading healthcare institution in the region dedicated to providing world-class medical services to patients from all walks of life.

Our hospital was established in 1958, with a mission to improve the health and well-being of our patients by providing exceptional medical care in a compassionate and respectful environment. Since then, we have been committed to providing the highest quality healthcare services to our patients, using the latest medical technologies and practices.

At Agha Khan Hospital, we offer a wide range of medical services, including diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of diseases, as well as surgical and non-surgical procedures. Our team of highly qualified medical professionals, including doctors, nurses, and support staff, is dedicated to delivering compassionate care to our patients

Our hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, including advanced diagnostic and treatment equipment, modern operating rooms, and comfortable patient rooms. We also have a team of highly skilled doctors who specialize in various fields, including cardiology, neurology, oncology, pediatrics, and many others.

At Agha Khan Hospital, we believe that every patient deserves the best care possible. We are committed to continuously improving our services and facilities to ensure that our patients receive the best possible care.
